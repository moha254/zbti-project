MESSAGE FROM THE HEAD OF ZU TVET CENTRE

Welcome to the Zetech Technical, Vocational Education and Training Centre, where we develop the finest and most skilled technical professionals through vocational education and training. Our programs range from certificate qualifiers to diploma programs that are designed to meet the needs of students at every level; from those just starting their careers to those looking to enhance their skills and knowledge. I am honoured to lead a team of passionate, qualified and competent faculty that provides high-quality academic, vocational, and social training that improves learning outcomes for all students and enables them to achieve their full potential and ultimately invent their future. Here, classroom learning is not our only focus. RECENT EVENTS

Huawei Technologies

Zetech University furthered its partnership with Huawei Technologies in an effort to enhance IT training and equip learners with the requisite skills to address national and global challenges through ICT. This comes in the wake of rapid digital growth in Kenya which is a key contributor to the expansion of the global digital economy in Africa.

Event Details
Research and Innovation Fair

The 5th annual Zetech University Research and Innovation Fair witnessed the highest number of exhibitors ever, with more than 20 corporates and individuals showcasing their brilliant innovations. Speaking during the event, Dr. Wahome Rureri who represented the Principal Secretary of State Department for University Education and Research lauded the University for the steps it has taken to facilitate research and innovation, highlighting that research is the hallmark of any globally successful university.

TECH FESTIVAL

Zetech University hosted the Ideas Tech festival at its Innovation labs, famously known as the iZET hub. The event, whose theme was leveraging on technology for sustainable socioeconomic development, was held on Thursday 25th May from 8 am to 5pm.Various participants showcased their ideas and innovations that fulfilled the theme of the event.
