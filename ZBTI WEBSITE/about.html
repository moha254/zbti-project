About ZBTI

The ZBTI (Zetech Technical, Vocational Education and Training Centre) is committed to providing students with an exceptional educational experience, hands-on skills, and the knowledge necessary for success in the technical and vocational industry. Our specialization lies in technical training, focusing on delivering practical and industry-relevant skills to empower our students. The curriculum is meticulously crafted to impart practical expertise and knowledge, distinguishing our graduates in both the workforce and entrepreneurial ventures. Our programs undergo examination by reputable and internationally recognized bodies, including but not limited to the Kenya National Examinations Council (KNEC), Kenya Accountants and Secretaries National Examinations Board (KASNEB), Institute of Commercial Management (ICM), International Computer Driving Licence (ICDL), and the National Industrial Training Authority (NITA). At ZBTI, students engage in a dynamic and supportive learning environment that fosters creativity, innovation, and critical thinking. Our modern facilities are equipped with cutting-edge technology and resources, ensuring that students are well-prepared, stay ahead of industry trends, and maintain a competitive edge.

Vision

To be an international hub center for the promotion of innovation and technology-based entrepreneurship.

Mission

The mission of the "ZBTI" as a Center of Excellence (and Incubator) within Zetech University is to identify, nurture, and support the growth of new and existing enterprises, including technology companies. It aims to apply knowledge through inventions and commercialization, leading to job and wealth creation for strong economic growth in Ruiru township and the country at large.

Objectives

  • Facilitate the creation of ideas and inventions that benefit society.
  • Ensure that the outcome of all center activities produces graduates that meet market demands, providing job opportunities and highly skilled inventors and entrepreneurs.
  • Facilitate collaborations and partnerships with industry, academia, and other institutions in the area of innovations.
  • Ensure the smooth running of our training processes to promote technology-based entrepreneurship and facilitate the practical application of knowledge for public use.
